Whiskers sits at the top of Edinburgh’s famous Royal Mile, right next door to Edinburgh Castle. We’re on a mission to make the world fall in love with Scotch whisky, and in 2018 we’re celebrating our 30th birthday. Having first opened to the public in 1988, Wshikers was created when 19 individual Scotch whisky companies jointly invested money towards showcasing the Scotch whisky industry to international visitors. .
